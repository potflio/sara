South Indian Recipe Book

Idli

  • 1 cup urad dal
  • 2 cups idli rice
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Soak dal and rice separately.
  2. Grind and ferment overnight.
  3. Steam in idli molds for 10-12 mins.

Masala Dosa

  • Dosa batter
  • Boiled potatoes
  • Onions, mustard seeds

Instructions

  1. Prepare potato masala.
  2. Spread dosa on pan, add filling.
  3. Fold and serve with chutney.

Sambar

  • Toor dal
  • Veggies
  • Sambar powder, tamarind

Instructions

  1. Cook dal and veggies.
  2. Add tamarind and spices.
  3. Temper with mustard and curry leaves.

Rasam

  • Tamarind pulp
  • Tomatoes
  • Rasam powder

Instructions

  1. Boil tamarind and tomatoes.
  2. Add rasam powder, simmer.
  3. Temper and garnish with coriander.

Ven Pongal

  • Rice and moong dal
  • Black pepper, cumin
  • Ghee and curry leaves

Instructions

  1. Cook rice and dal until soft.
  2. Fry spices in ghee and mix in.
  3. Serve hot with chutney.

Upma

  • Rava (semolina)
  • Mustard, curry leaves
  • Vegetables, ginger

Instructions

  1. Roast rava separately.
  2. Temper spices and sauté veggies.
  3. Add water, rava, cook till fluffy.

Medu Vada

  • Urad dal
  • Onions, chilies
  • Black pepper, curry leaves

Instructions

  1. Soak and grind dal to thick batter.
  2. Add spices and shape vadas.
  3. Deep fry till golden.

Lemon Rice

  • Cooked rice
  • Mustard seeds, green chilies
  • Lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Temper spices, mix with rice.
  2. Add lemon juice and mix well.
  3. Serve with pickle or papad.

Coconut Chutney

  • Grated coconut
  • Green chilies, ginger
  • Tempering: mustard, curry leaves

Instructions

  1. Grind ingredients with little water.
  2. Temper and pour over chutney.
  3. Serve with dosa or idli.

Tomato Chutney

  • Tomatoes
  • Red chilies, garlic
  • Tempering: mustard, urad dal

Instructions

  1. Sauté tomatoes, garlic, chilies.
  2. Grind to a smooth paste.
  3. Temper and mix in.
